About The Grand Buffet


Tradition since 1953

The Grand Buffet was founded in the city of Cagayan De Oro City in 1953 by Mr. Jade Aniñon, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for culinary excellence. Mr. Aniñon, a seasoned chef with years of experience in the culinary world, dreamed of creating a dining experience like no other, where guests could indulge in a wide array of mouthwatering dishes.

Over the years, The Grand Buffet became more than just a restaurant; it became a beloved institution woven into the fabric of Cagayan De Oro culture. Generations of families celebrated special occasions within its walls, creating cherished memories that would last a lifetime.
